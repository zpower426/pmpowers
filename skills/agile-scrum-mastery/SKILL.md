# Agile & Scrum Mastery: Agile Delivery and Rhythm Management

Agile is not about being fast, but about getting feedback earlier.

## Checklist

1. **Manage Backlog (Backlog Refinement)** — Ensure User Stories satisfy the INVEST principles.
2. **Run Sprint Planning** — Align goals, assess capacity, and determine the Sprint Backlog.
3. **Facilitate Daily Standups** — Focus on blockers rather than simple status reporting.
4. **Execute Sprint Review & Retrospective** — Demonstrate value and summarize process improvement items.

## Core Framework: INVEST Principles

- **Independent**: Decoupled from other stories.
- **Negotiable**: Leave room for discussion.
- **Valuable**: Delivers value to users.
- **Estimable**: Effort can be assessed.
- **Small**: Atomic (can be completed within one Sprint).
- **Testable**: Can be verified.

## Expert Principles

- **Defend the Sprint Goal**: During the Sprint, reject any non-urgent requirement injections.
- **Monitor Velocity Trends**: Focus on stability and consistency rather than just the absolute value.
- **Continuous Improvement**: Every retrospective must yield at least one concrete process optimization action item.
