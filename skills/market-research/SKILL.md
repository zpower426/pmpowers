# Market Research: Macro Research and Competitive Intelligence

Understand the battlefield to win the fight.

## Checklist

1. **Define Market Boundary** — In which segment are we competing? What are the TAM, SAM, and SOM?
2. **Execute Competitive Scan** — Who are the direct competitors? Who are the potential disruptors?
3. **Identify Industry Trends (Trend Spotting)** — Major changes in policy, technology, social, or economic levels.
4. **Synthesize Opportunity Space** — Where are the unmet gaps in the market?

## Competitive Analysis Framework: Adapted Five Forces

- **Existing Competition**: What is their core moat?
- **Threat of Substitutes**: Can users solve the problem in a completely different way?
- **New Entrants**: Which startups are trying to enter this space?
- **Bargaining Power of Suppliers/Customers**: Who holds the power in the industry chain?

## Delivery Standards
Research conclusions must be recorded in `docs/pmpowers/discovery/market-research.md` and directly linked to `opportunity-mapping`.
