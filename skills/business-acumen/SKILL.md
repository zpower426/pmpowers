# Business Acumen: Business Sensitivity and Profit Modeling

A product manager who doesn't make money is just doing charity.

## Checklist

1. **Analyze Business Model Canvas (BMC)** — Key partners, activities, resources, value propositions, channels, customers, costs, and revenues.
2. **Calculate Unit Economics** — Ensure LTV (Life Time Value) > 3 * CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost).
3. **Identify Market Positioning** — Are we a low-price leader, a differentiation pioneer, or a niche specialist?
4. **Assess Legal & Compliance Risks** — Ensure business practices comply with regulations and ethics.

## Expert Principles

- **Understand Cash Flow**: Know how every cent of the product is earned and where it is spent.
- **Ecosystem Thinking**: Reflect on how your product changes the distribution of interests in the industry upstream and downstream.
