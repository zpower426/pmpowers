# Roadmapping: Roadmap Planning and Evolution Rhythm

A roadmap is a phased deconstruction of the vision.

## Checklist

1. **Establish Time Horizons (Themes & Horizons)** — Now (Current Quarter), Next (Next Quarter), Later (Future).
2. **Align with Strategic Pillars** — Every roadmap item must be traceable back to a strategic pillar.
3. **Balance Features with Infrastructure** — Reserve 20% of time for handling technical debt and infrastructure.
4. **Stakeholder Review** — Ensure consensus on priorities among sales, operations, and R&D.

## Anti-Patterns

- **Promising Specific Dates**: A roadmap is not a Gantt chart. It should be based on "value delivery" rather than "time points."
- **Overcrowding**: Stuffing the roadmap so full that there is no buffer room.
- **Ignoring Outcomes**: A roadmap full of feature names without expected Outcomes.
