# QA & Quality Mindset: Quality Thinking and Acceptance Benchmarks

The PM is the ultimate gatekeeper of product quality.

## Checklist

1. **Perform User Acceptance Testing (UAT)** — Check each item strictly against the Acceptance Criteria (AC) in the Spec.
2. **Boundary & Negative Testing** — Attempt to "break" the product through illegal operations and extreme environments.
3. **Bug Triage & Technical Debt Assessment** — Decide which bugs must be fixed and which can be deferred.
4. **Verify Fault Tolerance** — When the system errors, does it provide friendly guidance instead of cryptic error codes?

## Core Framework: The Quality Triangle

- **Logicity**: Does it comply with business rules?
- **Usability**: Is the interaction natural? Is it intuitive?
- **Robustness**: Is it still safe and consistent in extreme cases?

## Expert Principles

Don't just do QA at the final stage. Quality should start from the moment the first line of a User Story is written.
