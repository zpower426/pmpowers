# Presentation & Storytelling: Product Narrative and Influence

Data convinces, but stories move.

## Checklist

1. **Conduct Audience Audit** — What do they care about? What is their level of understanding?
2. **Build Core Narrative (The Arc)** — Current State (Pain Points) -> Conflict (Obstacles) -> Solution (Product) -> Outcome (Vision).
3. **Design Visual Aids** — Use high signal-to-noise ratio slides or interactive prototypes (Visual Companion).
4. **Prepare Q&A Scripts** — Anticipate challenges and prepare data-driven responses.

## Expert Principles

- **One Key Point Per Slide**: Don't make the audience read the slide while trying to listen to you.
- **Anchor Value**: Every feature demonstration must connect to a real user value.
- **Passion and Confidence**: Your degree of belief in the product determines the audience's degree of belief.
