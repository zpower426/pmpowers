# GTM & Launch Management: Go-to-Market and Release Management

Launching is not equal to success; being perceived as valuable by users is success.

## Checklist

1. **Define Release Strategy (Phased vs. Big Bang)** — Canary testing, Beta invitations, or full-scale rollout?
2. **Align Internal Support** — Train sales/customer service, update FAQs, and prepare marketing collateral.
3. **Execute Smoke Tests & Rehearsals** — Simulate traffic and anomalies for the launch day.
4. **Post-launch Monitoring** — Keep a close eye on core metrics and negative user feedback.

## Expert Principles

- **Echo Chamber Warning**: Good internal test results do not guarantee market success. Pay attention to real cold-start users.
- **Feedback Loop**: In the first week after release, the PM should be on the front lines of customer service/community, listening directly to the "sound of gunfire."
- **Graceful Rollback Plan**: All releases must be equipped with an emergency rollback plan that can take effect within 5 minutes.
