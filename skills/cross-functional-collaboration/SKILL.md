# Cross-functional Collaboration: Collaboration and Consensus Building

Break down silos and establish a shared sense of purpose.

## Checklist

1. **Define RACI Matrix** — Clarify who is Responsible (R), Accountable (A), Consulted (C), and Informed (I).
2. **Establish Feedback Loops** — Set up fixed cross-departmental synchronization mechanisms.
3. **Eliminate Jargon Barriers** — Translate technical terms into business value and vice versa.
4. **Risk Warning Mechanism** — When one department encounters a blocker, how is a timely response from others ensured?

## Core Mandates

- **Contextual Consistency**: Ensure everyone understands "why we are doing this."
- **Transparency**: All decision-making processes and progress must be visible to the entire team.
- **Resolve Conflicts, Don't Evade**: Use metrics and experimental data to arbitrate cross-functional disagreements.
