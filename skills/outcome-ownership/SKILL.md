# Outcome Ownership: Result Ownership and Metric Accountability

What we want is "user problems solved," not "lines of code in the repository."

## Checklist

1. **Align with North Star Metrics** — After this feature goes live, what is the expected trend for the NSM?
2. **Execute Post-mortem Audits** — Thirty days after launch, have the original hypotheses been validated?
3. **Continuous Iteration or Sunset Decisions** — When metrics fall short, should we continue to optimize or decisively "stop loss"?
4. **Celebrate Value, Not Just Release** — When metrics are achieved, communicate the real business impact to the team.

## Core Mandates

- **No-Excuse Mindset**: R&D delays and market changes are challenges, but the PM is ultimately responsible for the Outcome.
- **Long-termism**: Reject suicidal growth that sacrifices long-term retention for short-term activity.
- **Learning is the Greatest Asset**: Even in failure, the accumulated knowledge and market understanding are valuable.
