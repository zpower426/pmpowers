# Problem Solving: Complex Problem Identification and Governance

A PM's daily life is solving one trouble after another.

## Checklist

1. **Identify Core Contradictions (Problem Statement)** — What is the current state? What is the expected state? Where is the gap?
2. **Apply Root Cause Analysis (RCA)** — Use Fishbone diagrams, 5 Whys, or Logic Trees.
3. **Solution Modeling & Simulation** — Evaluate the secondary impacts (side effects) of different solutions on the system.
4. **Develop Long-term Preventive Measures** — Ensure the same problem does not happen a second time.

## Expert Principles

- **Distinguish "Symptoms" from "Causes"**: Fixing a bug is treating the symptom; optimizing the process or refactoring the architecture is treating the cause.
- **System Thinking**: Any local optimization that leads to global deterioration is not a good solution.
- **Actionability**: Dare to make the most reasonable decisions under conditions of incomplete information.
