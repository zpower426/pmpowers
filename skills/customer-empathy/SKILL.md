# Customer Empathy: Deep Empathy and Experience Modeling

The first ability of a Product Manager is to "become the user."

## Checklist

1. **Build Empathy Map** — Describe what the user: Hears, Sees, Says & Does, Thinks & Feels.
2. **Identify Pains & Gains** — What do they fear? What do they desire?
3. **Map User Journey** — Plot the user emotion curve for every touchpoint.
4. **Identify Moments of Truth** — Which moments determine whether a user stays with or leaves the product?

## Core Framework: Empathy Map

| Dimension | Key Questions |
| --- | --- |
| **Say & Do** | What behaviors does the user exhibit in public? Are there contradictions? |
| **See & Hear** | How do the user's social environment, work environment, and friends influence them? |
| **Think & Feel** | What does the user really worry about late at night? What are their unexpressed aspirations? |

## Expert Principles

- **Reject Arrogance**: Don't say "the user is too stupid"; instead, ask "where did our design violate intuition?"
- **Extreme User Research**: Observe "power users" and "complete churners"; they provide the most extreme but valuable feedback.
