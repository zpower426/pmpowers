# Brainstorm: Product Brainstorming Command

Use this command to initiate a professional product design brainstorming session.

## Intent
Transform vague product ideas through `ABC` guidance into risk-assessed preliminary solutions.

## Trigger Actions
1. Activate the `brainstorming` skill.
2. Follow the `VDD` process for context analysis and solution comparison.
3. Ensure final deliverables are stored in `docs/pmpowers/specs/`.
